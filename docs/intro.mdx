# Welcome to AIHedge

AIHedge is a decentralized, non-custodial yield optimization protocol. Several entities and contributors participate in its development, adoption, and maintenance. As a result, it is helpful to distinguish the different areas of "AIHedge."

### The AIHedge Protocol
A decentralized, non-custodial yield optimization protocol implemented for the Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM) and built upon the Yearn V3 multi-strategy ERC-4626 vault standard.

### The AIHedge Interfaces
Multiple web interfaces, including the official dashboard at [dapp.aihedge.finance](https://dapp.aihedge.finance), allowing easy, self-custodial interaction with the underlying protocol.

### AIHedge Governance
A decentralized governance system enabled by the `AIHEDGE` token to manage protocol updates, vault strategies, and treasury allocation.

### AIHedge Contributors
The developers, machine learning researchers, and community members who build, audit, and promote the adoption of the AIHedge protocol and its neural network optimization strategies.

## AIHedge

AIHedge is a simple, automated yield routing layer designed to maximize risk-adjusted APYs in a permissionless, trustless manner. 

The protocol utilizes ERC-4626 smart contracts to facilitate simple integrations and programmatic interactions.

---

## Overview

AIHedge is a decentralized yield optimizer that automates the routing, rebalancing, and compounding of crypto assets (ERC-20 & ERC-4626 tokens) across premium DeFi protocols. Developed as an immutable base layer, the smart contracts ensure that depositors retain control of their funds while advanced strategies compound yields in perpetuity.

The smart contracts are non-custodial and designed to operate permissionlessly, serving as a reliable utility for individual yield seekers, automated vaults, and developer integrations.

---

## Key Concepts

Yield optimization in AIHedge involves:

* **AI-Powered Allocation**: Machine learning models (such as Reinforcement Learning PPO models) analyze yield options, gas costs, and liquidity in real-time to optimize capital allocation.
* **Curated Multi-Strategy Vaults**: Curators can construct custom yield vaults with specific asset distributions, guardrails, and risk specifications.
* **ERC-4626 Self-Custody**: Users retain 100% custody of their funds via vault share tokens, allowing permissionless deposit and withdrawal at any time.
* **Flexible Savings & Staking**: Seamless yield aggregation combining stablecoin savings products with Ethereum liquid staking strategies.
* **Open Integration**: Any DApp, smart contract, or institutional portal can integrate with AIHedge vaults to offer native yield features.
