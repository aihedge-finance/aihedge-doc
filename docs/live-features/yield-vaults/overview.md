# Vaults Marketplace

The Vaults Marketplace is the central hub of AIHedge — a curated listing of ERC-4626 multi-strategy yield vaults where you can browse, compare, and deposit assets to earn automated yield.

![Vaults Marketplace](/img/dapp/vaults_marketplace_1780574482429.png)

---

## What is a Vault?

A vault is a smart contract that accepts a single asset (e.g. USDC, ETH) and routes it across multiple underlying yield strategies simultaneously. Instead of manually managing positions, the vault does it for you:

- **Deposits** are immediately put to work earning yield
- **Rebalancing** happens automatically based on AI-optimized allocation
- **Yield compounds** within the vault over time
- **Withdrawals** can be made at any time — no lockup periods

---

## Reading the Marketplace

Each vault card in the marketplace shows:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Asset** | The token you deposit (e.g. USDC, WETH) |
| **APY** | Current annualized yield estimate |
| **TVL** | Total value locked across all depositors |
| **Network** | The chain this vault lives on |
| **Curator** | The strategy manager responsible for this vault |

---

## Vault Risk Levels

Vaults are categorized by their underlying strategy composition:

- **Conservative** — Primarily lending markets (Aave, Morpho). Low volatility, stable APY.
- **Balanced** — Mix of lending + liquidity provision. Moderate risk/reward.
- **Aggressive** — Higher-yield strategies including leverage or concentrated LP positions.

## Selecting a Vault

When evaluating a vault, consider:

1. **Asset match** — only deposit the asset the vault is designed for
2. **APY vs risk** — higher APY vaults typically carry higher underlying strategy risk
3. **TVL size** — larger TVL generally indicates more established, battle-tested vaults
4. **Curator reputation** — check the curator's track record and strategy description

Click any vault to open its detail page, which shows the full strategy breakdown, historical APY chart, and deposit/withdraw interface.
