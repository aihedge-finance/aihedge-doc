# Staking & Unstaking

This guide walks you through staking ETH for liquid staking tokens (LSTs) and redeeming them back to ETH via the AIHedge DApp.

---

## Before You Start

- ✅ Wallet connected on **Ethereum Mainnet**
- ✅ ETH available in your wallet (plus a small amount for gas)

---

## How to Stake ETH

1. **Go to Liquid Staking** — In the DApp sidebar, navigate to the **Liquid Staking** section.

2. **Select a protocol** — Choose your preferred liquid staking provider (e.g. Ether.fi or Lido).

3. **Enter an amount** — Type how much ETH you want to stake, or click **Max**.

4. **Stake** — Click **Stake** and confirm the transaction in your wallet.

5. **Receive your LST** — Once confirmed, you'll receive the corresponding liquid staking token (e.g. `weETH` or `wstETH`) in your wallet. This token automatically accrues staking rewards.

![Stake ETH Interface](/img/dapp/liquid_staking_stake_1780574589389.png)

---

## How to Unstake (Redeem)

**Option A — Instant redemption via secondary market**

Some LSTs (like `wstETH`) can be swapped back to ETH instantly on DEXs like Curve or Uniswap at near 1:1 rates. This is the fastest method.

**Option B — Native withdrawal (via protocol)**

1. **Go to Liquid Staking → Unstake tab** in the DApp.
2. **Enter the LST amount** you want to redeem.
3. **Initiate withdrawal** — Click **Unstake** and confirm in your wallet.
4. **Wait for processing** — Native withdrawals go through Ethereum's withdrawal queue. Processing time varies (typically a few days depending on queue length).
5. **Claim ETH** — Once processed, return to the DApp to claim your ETH.

![Unstake ETH Interface](/img/dapp/liquid_staking_unstake_1780574610387.png)

---

## Notes

- **No minimum stake** — You can stake any amount of ETH.
- **Rewards accrue automatically** — Your LST balance increases (or its exchange rate to ETH increases) over time as staking rewards are earned; no manual claiming is needed.
- **Gas fees apply** — Both staking and unstaking require on-chain transactions.
