# Available Products

Flexible Earn currently offers savings pools for the following assets. All products are available on supported networks with no minimum deposit requirement.

![Flexible Earn Pools](/img/dapp/flexible_earn_pools_1780575195019.png)

---

## Stablecoin Savings

### USDC Savings
- **Asset:** USD Coin (USDC)
- **Underlying:** Morpho / Aave USDC lending markets
- **APY:** Variable — follows real-time lending demand
- **Network:** Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum

### USDT Savings
- **Asset:** Tether (USDT)
- **Underlying:** Aave USDT lending markets
- **APY:** Variable
- **Network:** Ethereum, BNB Chain

### DAI / sDAI
- **Asset:** DAI (MakerDAO)
- **Underlying:** MakerDAO DSR (DAI Savings Rate) + Morpho
- **APY:** Partially fixed (DSR floor) + variable top-up
- **Network:** Ethereum

---

## How to Deposit into a Pool

1. **Navigate to Flexible Earn** in the DApp sidebar.
2. **Select a product** (e.g. USDC Savings).
3. **Enter the amount** you want to save.
4. **Approve & Deposit** — approve the token spend (first time only), then confirm the deposit.
5. **Earn** — yield starts accruing immediately.

![Flexible Earn Deposit Interface](/img/dapp/flexible_earn_deposit_1780575224610.png)

---

## Withdrawing from a Pool

1. Open the product page for your active savings pool.
2. Click the **Withdraw** tab.
3. Enter the amount to withdraw (or click **Max** for your full balance).
4. Confirm the transaction — funds return to your wallet instantly.

---

## Notes

- APYs displayed are **current rates** and will fluctuate with lending market supply and demand.
- All pools are backed by audited protocols (Morpho, Aave, MakerDAO).
- There are no fees charged by AIHedge on Flexible Earn products at this time.
