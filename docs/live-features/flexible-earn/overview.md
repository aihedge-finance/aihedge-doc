# Flexible Savings Overview

Flexible Earn is a savings-style product on AIHedge designed for users who want stable, predictable yield on stablecoins and blue-chip assets — without locking funds or managing complex strategies.

![Flexible Earn Overview](/img/dapp/flexible_earn_overview_1780575182114.png)

---

## What is Flexible Earn?

Flexible Earn aggregates yield from leading DeFi lending markets (primarily Morpho and Aave) and presents them as simple savings products. Think of it like a high-yield savings account — but on-chain, permissionless, and self-custodial.

Key properties:
- **No lockup** — deposit and withdraw at any time
- **Stable APY** — optimized for consistent returns rather than volatile high yields
- **Single-asset** — deposit one asset, earn yield in that same asset
- **Auto-compounding** — yield is reinvested automatically

---

## How it Works

1. You deposit a supported stable asset (e.g. USDC, USDT, DAI) into a Flexible Earn pool.
2. AIHedge routes your funds across the best available lending market rates (Morpho, Aave) in real time.
3. Interest accrues block-by-block and is automatically compounded.
4. You can withdraw your full balance (principal + yield) at any time.

---

## Who is it For?

Flexible Earn is ideal for:

- **Stablecoin holders** looking to put idle assets to work
- **Risk-averse users** who prioritize capital preservation over maximum yield
- **DeFi beginners** who want a simple, one-click yield experience without navigating complex protocols

## Difference from Yield Vaults

| | Flexible Earn | Yield Vaults |
|---|---|---|
| **Asset type** | Stablecoins & blue-chips | Broader range |
| **Strategy complexity** | Simple (lending only) | Multi-strategy |
| **APY profile** | Stable, lower | Variable, higher potential |
| **Risk level** | Conservative | Conservative to Aggressive |
| **Lockup** | None | None |
